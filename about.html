About Dua Computers

A computer shop on Computer Street, Ruwi — new and used devices, and repairs done on-site.

What we do

Dua Computers sells new and used laptops, desktops, printers, monitors, and accessories, alongside full repair and servicing for laptops, PCs, PS4/PS5 consoles, and printers — including data recovery and software support.

We're based right in Ruwi, on Computer Street, so you can walk in, see the device before you buy it, and get repairs handled without shipping anything off-site.

Why buy from us

Every used device is checked before it goes on the shelf. Prices are listed upfront on our shop page — no back-and-forth needed unless you want more detail, which is exactly what WhatsApp is for.

Whether you're after a new machine, a budget-friendly used one, or just need something fixed, it's all handled in one place.
